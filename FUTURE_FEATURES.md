# Future Features to Explore

Features identified from competitive research (Focus Friend, Opal, Jomo, BePresent, Brick, and others) that are not in the current scope but worth revisiting.

## Tier 2 (Strong Nice-to-Haves)

- **Daily/weekly challenges and achievement badges** -- e.g., "Use social media < 30 min today" or "Complete 3 deep focus sessions this week" (BePresent, Clearspace)
- **Calendar-triggered focus modes** -- auto-trigger focus modes based on calendar events (Freedom)
- **Open limits** -- limit how many times per day you can open an app, e.g., only 5 opens of Instagram (Opal)
- **Progressive friction on bypass attempts** -- each unlock attempt in a day gets a longer delay (ScreenZen)
- **Before/after comparison stats** -- show screen time improvement since installing the app (Jomo)
- **Doomscroll awareness reminders** -- hourly scrolling awareness nudges (BePresent)

## Tier 3 (Differentiators)

- **Accountability partner / social features** -- a trusted friend gets notified of screen time or can intervene (Sheppie, Whistl)
- **Group focus sessions** -- multiple users focus together with shared timers (Flipd, Flora)
- **Leaderboards** -- compete with friends on focus time or screen time reduction (Opal, BePresent)
- **Ambient sounds during focus** -- rain, cafe noise, nature sounds during sessions (Habi, Forest)
- **Location-based auto-blocking** -- auto-activate focus mode when arriving at work/school via geofencing (Freedom)
- **Apple Watch companion** -- start/stop sessions, view stats, haptic reminders (FocusFit)
- **Unbypassable mode option** -- literally cannot be turned off once started, for extreme discipline (Opal Deep Focus, Cold Turkey, BePresent BEAST MODE)
- **Gamification (XP, badges, virtual garden)** -- earn XP, level up, grow virtual plants during focus (Forest, BePresent)
- **Real tree planting** -- focus sessions fund actual trees via partnerships (Forest, Flora)
- **Exercise-gated access** -- AI-verified push-ups/squats to unlock screen time (RepsForReels)
- **Step-based unlocking** -- walk X steps to unlock social media (Jomo)
- **Real-life rewards** -- redeem focus points for subscriptions like Headspace, ClassPass (BePresent)
- **Monetary stakes** -- pledge money you forfeit if you fail your goal (BePresent)
- **Friend-controlled locks** -- give someone else the ability to lock/unlock your apps (Friend Controls)
- **Emotional state tracking** -- log how you feel before/after using apps to identify triggers (Attentive)
- **Sleep quality correlation** -- correlate screen time patterns with sleep data via HealthKit (OffScreen)
- **Morning routine enforcement** -- won't let you use phone until morning routine is complete (LOCKD)
